If you no longer wish to use lineage, you can delete your account and disconnect from Open Humans.

Delete account

Click the button below to delete your account and the following:

  • Data imported from your Open Humans account
  • Data uploaded by you to the Web App
  • Data generated through your use of the Web App

Note that information the Web App automatically collects will be retained per the Data Use Policy.

Disconnect lineage from Open Humans

To disconnect lineage from Open Humans, you can remove the connection here.

De-authorizing the project will also attempt to automatically delete your account and data (same effect as clicking the Delete Account button).

Note that data uploaded by lineage to your Open Humans account will still exist, unless you request that it be deleted.
